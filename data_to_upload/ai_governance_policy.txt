AI Governance Policy - Orion Technologies

Purpose
The AI Governance framework ensures that AI systems are designed and deployed responsibly.

Key Principles

Transparency
AI systems must provide explanations for automated decisions affecting customers.

Accountability
Each AI system must have an assigned system owner responsible for its performance and compliance.

Bias Monitoring
AI models must be periodically audited for bias across protected demographic groups.

Human Oversight
Critical business decisions must include a human-in-the-loop review mechanism.

Data Governance Integration
AI systems must only use approved datasets managed under the Enterprise Data Governance Program.

Risk Categories

Low Risk
Internal automation tools.

Medium Risk
AI systems assisting employees in decision making.

High Risk
Customer-facing systems impacting financial or legal outcomes.
