# Sample data for testing

Place sample PDF, TXT, CSV, or XLSX files here to upload via the UI or API.

Example **TXT** content you can save as `benefits.txt`:

```
Employee Benefits Policy

Eligibility: Full-time employees are eligible for benefits after 90 days of continuous employment.

Health insurance: Coverage begins on the first day of the month following the eligibility date.

Leave: Employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year. Unused PTO may roll over up to 5 days.
```

Then upload via the app and ask: "When do benefits start?" or "How much PTO do employees get?"
